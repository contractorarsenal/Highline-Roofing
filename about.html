Who We Are

Three Generations.
One Standard of Excellence.

Highline Builders & Roofing is a third-generation, family-owned roofing company with deep roots in Everett. What started as a trade passed down through our family has grown into a trusted local company built on doing the work right.

With over 100 years of combined experience, we have handled everything from minor leak repairs to full roof replacements after major storm damage. That experience helps us diagnose problems quickly and recommend the right fix.

Being family owned and operated still means something here. You work with the people responsible for the work, the communication, and the final result. No call-center handoff. No franchise script.

We serve Everett, Bothell, Mill Creek, Lynnwood, Marysville, Snohomish, Mukilteo, and Lake Stevens, and we treat every project like it is in our own neighborhood.
